Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
  • How long does it take to deliver the cake?
    • For classic cakes, your cake will be delivered in 1-2 working days.
    For custom cakes, your cake will be delivered in 1-5 working days.

  • How long can we keep the cakes?
    • We recommend consuming the cake within 48hours of delivery.

  • What sizes of cakes are available?
    • For classic cakes, we only have one size of 8inches (6 slices).
    For custom cakes, we have 2 different sizes of cakes to choose from:
    Regular 8inches (6 slices) or Large 11inches (8 slices).
